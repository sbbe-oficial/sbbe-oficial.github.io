Principles and Social Objectives


Art. 3

The BSEB adopts the following principles:

I. Absolute commitment to the scientific method and the use of scientific evidence as a guiding principle for social conduct;

II. Visibility for social minorities;

III. Gender equality;

IV. Respect for diversity;

V. Inclusion;

VI. Ethics;

VII. Efficiency;

VIII. Cooperation;

IX. Financial transparency;

XI. Dissemination and instrumentalization of research produced;

XII. Promotion of innovative scientific production.

Art. 4

The objectives of BSEB are:

I. Bring together and promote communication among scholars in the field of Evolutionary Biology;

II. Promote and encourage activities and studies related to Evolutionary Biology;

III. Stimulate contact with international and national societies with similar purposes;

IV. Represent Brazilian evolutionary biologists to the National and International Scientific Community, as well as to governmental and private entities;

V. Promote the dissemination of knowledge of Evolutionary Biology to Brazilian society, including the promotion of scientific evidence in Evolutionary Biology as a guide for public policies;

VI. Encourage the creation of Evolutionary Biology courses at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels.

Â§1 To achieve the above objectives, BSEB may:

a) Produce, publish, distribute, and disseminate articles, books, journals, videos, films, photos, and similar materials;

b) Document, through all means, its various activities, as well as facts and situations related to its purposes;

c) Enter into contracts and agreements and/or associate with other natural or legal persons, public or private, provided that SBBE does not subordinate itself to the partner;

d) Raise financial resources from donors, whether natural or legal persons, members or non-members;

e) Develop means of communication in various media to promote its actions and disseminate its research;

f) Establish institutional relationships and affiliations within the limits of its objectives with other associations and companies;

g) Hold regular meetings for the presentation and discussion of works and to foster the exchange of ideas among its members;

h) Organize scientific events (congresses, symposia, conferences, workshops), where its members will gather to discuss presented works;

i) Promote and sponsor the formation of regional nuclei of this association;

j) Organize popularization conferences, scientific dissemination, and various Evolutionary Biology-related courses;

k) Provide financial aid for the training of evolutionary biologists nationally and internationally;

l) Carry out other activities consistent with the statutory objectives.

Â§2 The actions specified in items H, I, and K above will be detailed and regulated for the members through a document produced and disseminated by the Administrative Board.

Art. 5 In carrying out its activities, BSEB will not allow any type of discrimination for any reason, prioritizing ethics and the realization of human rights, which is fundamental for the effective functioning of society.

VI. Voluntary withdrawal request.

Sole Paragraph: Except for items IV and V, in cases described in this article, the member has the right to defense and appeal to the General Assembly.
